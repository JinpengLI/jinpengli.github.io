Finger Detection using Webcam (Object Detection)

Capture

PDF, Demo, Source and binary

[c] Jinpeng Li and Yinwei Zhan, Reversi Game Using Vision Interaction (Finger Detection), 2008. PDF

This work elaborates a vision-based interface (virtual touch screen) via an inexpensive webcam and an ordinary monitor for reversi game. Three main steps, calibration, segmentation and finding the finger tip, are presented in our model. Compared with GUI-based methods which is an efficient and effective way for office use and the classical intuitive methods using expensive device such as wearable camera, the simple model we propose is capable of efficacious.

[a] Jinpeng Li, Lifu Wu, Xu Lin, Interactive 3D Digital Map (using OpenGL), First Prize in15th Guangdong College Cup of Software Design Contest, among 14 Universities in Guangdong, China, Nov. 2005.

[b] Liangfeng Chen, Jinpeng Li and Minglin Zhang, 3D Engine based on Network (using Direct X), Innovation Award and First Prize in 16th Guangdong College Cup of Software Design Contest, among 14 Universities in Guangdong, China, Nov. 2006.

The idea of those two works is to integrate 3D programming effects (most of them for a 3D map) as a 3D engine for developers so that the other developers can be built their works based on our engine. [b] is the successor of [a]. We added many functionalies to [b], including network communications, real water simulation, reflection, etc. Our works [a] and [b] won two champions for two successive years in "Guangdong College Cup of Software Design Contest" among 14 Universities in Guangdong, China.
